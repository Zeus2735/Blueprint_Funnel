Master Recruitment Your Way: The Self-Paced Course

Take control of your basketball recruitment journey with our comprehensive Self-Paced Course. Designed for motivated athletes and parents, this program provides all the tools, knowledge, and resources you need to navigate the complex world of college recruitment and NIL opportunities at your own speed.

What You'll Get

  • Complete Video Training Library: Access hours of expert video content covering every aspect of recruitment, from building your profile to communicating with coaches and understanding eligibility.
  • Recruitment Strategy Workbook: A step-by-step interactive workbook to help you define your goals, identify target schools, craft your outreach messages, and track your progress.
  • College Coach Database Access: Gain access to our curated database of college coaches across different divisions, making it easier to find the right contacts for your target schools.
  • Monthly Group Q&A Sessions: Join live online sessions with recruitment experts and fellow athletes to ask questions, share experiences, and get timely advice.

Benefits

  • Learn and implement strategies on your own schedule.
  • Gain lifetime access to valuable video content and resources.
  • Develop a clear, actionable recruitment plan using the workbook.
  • Efficiently find and contact college coaches.
  • Connect with a community and get answers in monthly Q&A sessions.
  • Build a strong foundation for your athletic and academic future.
